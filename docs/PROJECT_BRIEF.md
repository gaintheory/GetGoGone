# GetGoGone Project Brief

GetGoGone is a full-stack marketing hub for small used car dealers, starting with a Buy Here Pay Here lot that currently manages inventory and leads through Carsforsale/MyCommandCenter and primarily advertises on Facebook.

The product goal is to expand the dealership's marketing surface area beyond the current workflow by making it easy for all salespeople to create, reuse, publish, and track vehicle campaigns across practical channels.

Core outcomes:
- Import or sync inventory from the current source of truth.
- Turn each vehicle into reusable campaign packages.
- Generate English and Spanish ads, replies, and creatives.
- Support paid Meta ads, organic/social posting workflows, Google Business Profile, Craigslist, LinkedIn commercial vehicle campaigns, email, SMS, and future paid channels.
- Track leads, activity, tasks, and campaign performance.
- Build a repeatable product that can later serve additional small dealers.
