# Feature Roadmap

Build tracks:
1. App foundation and design system
2. Inventory import/sync
3. Vehicle campaign hub
4. Creative builder
5. Bilingual ad/message generation
6. Marketing composer for SMS/email
7. Lead management and lead events
8. Tasks and workflows
9. Reports and attribution
10. Integrations and publishing support

Primary principle:
Build full product capability, but keep each module independently usable and testable.
