# Chrysler Brand Guide & Copy Model Knowledge

This brand guide outlines key selling points, typical model configurations (years 2011-2022), and compliant, persuasive copy angles for Chrysler inventory.

---

## 1. Brand Identity: Family Space & Premium Comfort
Chrysler is synonymous with **family comfort, spacious utility, and smooth highway cruisers**. For a BHPH buyer, a Chrysler vehicle (especially a minivan like the Pacifica or Town & Country) is often the ultimate answer to transport challenges for large families. It sips highway gas, keeps the kids separated and quiet, and fits all their belongings.

### Key Selling Points:
- **Stow 'n Go Seating:** The industry standard for utility. Rear seats fold flat into the floor in seconds to convert the passenger cabin into a cargo bed.
- **V6 Power & Efficiency:** The 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine is robust, has plenty of passing power, and maintains high fuel economy on road trips.
- **High comfort for Passengers:** Multiple charging ports, rear climate control, and cup holders galore.

---

## 2. Core Models & Copy Angles

### Model A: Chrysler Pacifica (Family Minivan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2017 - 2022
*   **Key Trims:** LX (standard), Touring / Touring L (popular family choices), Limited (luxury)
*   **Key Specs:** 3.6L Pentastar V6 (smooth, reliable power)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Smart Parent Choice:* Emphasize the Stow 'n Go seats, backup camera, large touchscreen, and rear air conditioning.
    *   *Road Trip Comfort:* Highlight the smooth highway ride and low noise levels.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Make family travel stress-free. This Pacifica offers Stow 'n Go fold-flat seating, active backup safety, and cold rear AC for everyone."*

### Model B: Chrysler Town & Country (Value Minivan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2011 - 2016
*   **Key Specs:** 3.6L Pentastar V6 (bulletproof family engine)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *High-Utility Budget Van:* Standard leather seats, power sliding doors, Stow 'n Go, and exceptional room at an ultra-low purchase cost.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Get massive family utility for an affordable payment. This Town & Country offers power doors, leather seating, Stow 'n Go flexibility, and cold rear AC."*

### Model C: Chrysler 300 (Full-Size Premium Sedan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2011 - 2021
*   **Key Specs:** 3.6L V6 (excellent fuel economy for its size) or 5.7L Hemi V8
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *Bold Premium Luxury:* The iconic American full-size sedan. Highlight the massive rear seat room, elegant chrome styling, quiet highway ride, and powerful presence.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Travel in premium comfort and style. This Chrysler 300 offers a whisper-quiet highway ride, a massive interior cabin, and bold styling."*
