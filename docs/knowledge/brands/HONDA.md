# Honda Brand Guide & Copy Model Knowledge

This brand guide outlines key selling points, typical model configurations (years 2010-2022), and compliant, persuasive copy angles for Honda inventory.

---

## 1. Brand Identity: The Reliability & Resale Standard
For BHPH buyers, a Honda is a top-tier premium vehicle. It is viewed as a highly desirable, extremely reliable investment. Buyers choose Honda because they know it will start every morning, require minimal repairs, and hold its value if they ever need to trade it in.

### Key Selling Points:
- **Bulletproof Durability:** Famous for running past 200,000 miles with basic oil changes and standard maintenance.
- **Top-Tier Resale Value:** Holds its equity better than almost any other brand, protecting the buyer's investment.
- **Smart Engineering:** Excellent fuel efficiency combined with spacious, highly practical cabins.

---

## 2. Core Models & Copy Angles

### Model A: Honda Accord (Midsize Sedan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020 (mostly 9th and 10th generation)
*   **Key Trims:** LX (standard), EX (sunroof/alloys), EX-L (leather), Sport (sport styling/paddles)
*   **Key Specs:** 2.4L I4 or 1.5T I4 (highly efficient), 3.5L V6 (smooth power)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Premium Commuter:* Focus on the smooth highway ride, massive back seat legroom, and fuel efficiency.
    *   *Dignity Drive:* Highlight that you don't have to sacrifice style or comfort to get an approved car.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Need a commuter that feels like a luxury ride but sips gas? This Accord Sport gives you sleek looks, cold AC, and Honda's famous reliability."*

### Model B: Honda Civic (Compact Sedan/Coupe)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2021
*   **Key Trims:** DX/LX (basic commuter), EX (sunroof, tech), Touring (premium)
*   **Key Specs:** 1.8L I4 or 2.0L I4 (ultra-low maintenance, sips fuel)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Gas-Saver Hero:* Focus on low cost to fill up and bulletproof commuter reputation.
    *   *The Smart Budget Choice:* Highlight that it's easy to park, cheap to insure, and lasts forever.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Beat the gas pumps with the ultimate commuter. This Civic LX is famous for low maintenance, high MPG, and running forever on basic care."*

### Model C: Honda CR-V (Compact SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020
*   **Key Trims:** LX (utility), EX (safety/sunroof), EX-L (leather)
*   **Key Specs:** 2.4L I4, RealTime AWD optional (excellent for winter weather)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Family Protector:* Focus on excellent safety scores, high seating position, and flexible cargo space.
    *   *Weather-Ready AWD:* Highlight safety in rain or snow.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Ready for school drop-offs and weekend grocery runs? This CR-V AWD gives your family high safety ratings, tons of cargo space, and winter-ready traction."*

### Model D: Honda Pilot (3rd Row SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2019
*   **Key Trims:** LX, EX, EX-L, Touring
*   **Key Specs:** 3.5L V6 (durable power), 8-passenger capacity
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Ultimate Family Hauler:* Focus on 8-passenger seating, rear entertainment (if equipped), and massive towing/storage capacity.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Need room for the whole crew? This Pilot seats up to 8 in comfort with cold rear AC and Honda's bulletproof V6 power."*
