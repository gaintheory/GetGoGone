# Jeep Brand Guide & Copy Model Knowledge

This brand guide outlines key selling points, typical model configurations (years 2011-2022), and compliant, persuasive copy angles for Jeep inventory.

---

## 1. Brand Identity: The Lifestyle & Off-Road Icon
Jeep represents freedom, adventure, and outdoor fun. Unlike buyers choosing sedans out of pure necessity, a Jeep buyer is often highly motivated by **style and lifestyle identity**. They want to feel excited about their car, even while working through credit rebuild programs.

### Key Selling Points:
- **Legendary 4x4 Capabilities:** Designed for rain, mud, or snow, providing safety and confidence.
- **Rugged, Distinct Styling:** Instant curb appeal and adventure-ready stance.
- **Proven Powertrains:** The 3.6L Pentastar V6 is one of the most reliable and highly produced V6 engines in American history.

---

## 2. Core Models & Copy Angles

### Model A: Jeep Wrangler Unlimited (Lifestyle SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020 (JK and JL generations)
*   **Key Trims:** Sport (rugged basic), Sahara (painted fenders/comfort), Rubicon (extreme off-road)
*   **Key Specs:** 3.6L V6 Pentastar (highly durable), 4-Door configuration (Unlimited)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Ultimate Freedom Vehicle:* Emphasize the removable top/doors, standard 4x4, and iconic look.
    *   *High Equity Protection:* Wranglers hold their resale value better than almost any other vehicle in the world.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Maneja el vehículo más icónico del mundo. Este Wrangler Unlimited te ofrece tracción 4x4, techo removible y un valor de reventa que protege tu inversión."*

### Model B: Jeep Grand Cherokee (Midsize Luxury SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2011 - 2021 (WK2 generation)
*   **Key Trims:** Laredo (standard luxury), Limited (leather/tech), Overland / Summit (premium)
*   **Key Specs:** 3.6L Pentastar V6 or 5.7L Hemi V8
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Premium SUV Solution:* Emphasize the luxurious interior, high tow capacity, and smooth highway ride.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Get premium comfort and standard Jeep toughness. This Grand Cherokee Laredo offers dual-zone climate controls, a smooth V6 engine, and a spacious cargo area."*

### Model C: Jeep Compass / Cherokee / Renegade (Compact Crossovers)
*   **Typical Years:** 2014 - 2021
*   **Key Specs:** 2.4L Tigershark I4 (decent fuel economy), 3.2L V6 (Cherokee)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Budget-Friendly SUV:* Perfect blend of fuel economy, style, and winter-ready 4x4 capability at a low entry cost.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Ready for winter slick? This Compass 4x4 gives you Jeep's famous safety traction, fuel-efficient I4 power, and modern tech in a compact size."*
