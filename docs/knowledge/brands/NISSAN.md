# Nissan Brand Guide & Copy Model Knowledge

This brand guide outlines key selling points, typical model configurations (years 2012-2022), and compliant, persuasive copy angles for Nissan inventory.

---

## 1. Brand Identity: Modern Style & High Value
For BHPH buyers, Nissan represents modern tech, comfortable cabin design, and high-end features at a highly affordable price point. Buyers choose Nissan because they get a modern-looking, comfortable vehicle (usually equipped with Bluetooth, backup cameras, and keyless start) for a lower entry cost than a comparable Honda or Toyota.

### Key Selling Points:
- **Zero Gravity Seats:** Celebrated for incredible seating comfort, making long commutes easy.
- **Advanced Standard Tech:** Nissan is known for putting backup cameras and Bluetooth in base trims earlier than competitors.
- **High-Value Entry:** Exceptional features-to-price ratio, offering great value for money.

---

## 2. Core Models & Copy Angles

### Model A: Nissan Rogue / Rogue Sport (Crossover SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2014 - 2021
*   **Key Trims:** S (basic utility), SV (alloys/push button), SL (leather/luxury)
*   **Key Specs:** 2.5L I4 (Rogue) or 2.0L I4 (Rogue Sport), AWD optional
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Comfortable Crossover:* Highlight the rear storage space (Divide-N-Hide system), backup camera, and smooth, soft ride.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Need a modern crossover with a comfortable ride? This Rogue SV offers push-button start, a backup camera, huge cargo space, and exceptional fuel economy."*

### Model B: Nissan Altima / Sentra (Sedans)
*   **Typical Years:** 2012 - 2021
*   **Key Trims:** S (commuter), SV (tech convenience), SR (sport styling)
*   **Key Specs:** 1.8L I4 (Sentra), 2.5L I4 (Altima commuter), 3.5L V6 (Altima power)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Sleek Highway Cruiser:* Focus on the sleek modern exterior, spacious cabins, and excellent highway fuel efficiency (35+ MPG).
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Cut down your weekly fuel bill. This Altima sips gas on the highway, sips regular fuel, and offers a super roomy interior for your family."*

### Model C: Nissan NV200 (Compact Cargo Van)
*   **Typical Years:** 2013 - 2020
*   **Key Specs:** 2.0L I4 (highly efficient, work-ready cargo)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Small Business Partner:* Perfect for local deliveries, locksmiths, florists, and mobile service businesses. High reliability, low cost to run.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Save money while growing your business. This NV200 cargo van offers massive cargo utility, fits into tight city spaces, and sips regular gas."*

### Model D: Nissan Frontier (Midsize Truck)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020
*   **Key Trims:** SV, PRO-4X
*   **Key Specs:** 4.0L V6 (extremely durable, traditional work truck engine)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Unbreakable Work Truck:* The Frontier is famous for keeping the same simple, bulletproof V6 chassis and mechanical parts for over a decade. Emphasize durability.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Need a work truck that never quits? This Frontier SV offers robust V6 towing power, standard bed hauling utility, and classic bulletproof durability."*
