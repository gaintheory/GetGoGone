# Toyota Brand Guide & Copy Model Knowledge

This brand guide outlines key selling points, typical model configurations (years 2010-2022), and compliant, persuasive copy angles for Toyota inventory.

---

## 1. Brand Identity: The Gold Standard of Dependability
In the BHPH universe, a Toyota is the most highly sought-after brand. It represents the absolute pinnacle of mechanical reliability. A buyer choosing a Toyota is buying peace of mind — they want zero unexpected mechanic bills, ultra-low fuel costs, and a vehicle that is virtually indestructible.

### Key Selling Points:
- **Bulletproof Engines & Transmissions:** World-renowned for easily reaching 300,000 miles with basic care.
- **Cheapest Long-Term Maintenance:** Parts are widely available, cheap, and simple to repair.
- **Superior Value Retention:** Holds its equity better than any other brand on the market.

---

## 2. Core Models & Copy Angles

### Model A: Toyota Camry (Midsize Sedan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020
*   **Key Trims:** LE (standard comfort), SE (sport wheels/grille), XLE (leather/luxury)
*   **Key Specs:** 2.5L I4 (legendary reliability), 3.5L V6 (smooth highway pass)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Commuter Standard:* Highlight the quiet cabin, smooth suspension, and massive interior space.
    *   *Unbeatable Peace of Mind:* Remind the buyer they are choosing the most reliable sedan ever built.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Drive with absolute confidence. This Camry LE is the gold standard of reliability — offering a smooth highway ride, low fuel costs, and legendary longevity."*

### Model B: Toyota Corolla (Compact Sedan)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2021
*   **Key Trims:** L/LE (basic gas-saver), S/SE (sportier trim)
*   **Key Specs:** 1.8L I4 (ultra-simple, bulletproof engine, sips regular gas)
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Gas-Saving Workhorse:* Emphasize the ultra-low fuel consumption and lack of repair costs.
    *   *Perfect First Car or Ride-Share:* Low insurance, easy parking, runs forever.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Keep your hard-earned money in your wallet. This Corolla LE is sips gas, costs next to nothing to maintain, and is ready for your daily commute."*

### Model C: Toyota RAV4 (Compact SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2020
*   **Key Trims:** LE (utility), XLE (sunroof/alloys), Limited (leather/tech)
*   **Key Specs:** 2.5L I4, AWD optional
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *The Practical Family Adventure:* Emphasize the high ride height, massive rear cargo space, and Toyota safety sense features (on newer models).
    *   *Weather-Ready Utility:* Mention AWD safety in rain or winter slick.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Upgrade your family's ride to a RAV4. Get AWD peace of mind, a huge cargo area for groceries or gear, and Toyota's legendary dependability."*

### Model D: Toyota Highlander (3rd Row SUV)
*   **Typical Years:** 2010 - 2019
*   **Key Trims:** LE, XLE, Limited
*   **Key Specs:** 2.7L I4 or 3.5L V6, 3rd row seating
*   **Copy Angles:**
    *   *Premium Family Comfort:* Focus on 3rd row seat flexibility, V6 towing power, and top-tier family safety ratings.
    *   *Example Hook:* *"Room for the whole family and all their gear. This Highlander XLE gives you V6 power, comfortable 3rd-row seating, and Toyota's bulletproof durability."*
